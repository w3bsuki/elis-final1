         HOW TO FILL THIS TEMPLATE
================================================

1. Read through the entire document before starting to fill it out
2. Replace the blank lines (____) with your content
3. For sections where multiple items are needed (like books or services), copy and paste the template section to add more as needed
4. Save your changes frequently
5. For images, provide file names that clearly indicate what they are (e.g., "book1-cover.jpg")
6. Text lengths are suggestions - you can write more or less as needed
7. If a section doesn't apply to you, you can mark it as "N/A"
